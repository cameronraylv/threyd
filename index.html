No extraction. No exploitation. Just connections that hold.

Questions

FAQ

What does "fully managed" actually mean?

It means we own the operation, not just the placement. We recruit and onboard your team, manage day-to-day performance, handle escalations, and deliver weekly reporting on the metrics that matter. You set the standard. We hold it.

How do you measure performance?

We establish KPIs at the start of every engagement — tailored to your operation, not borrowed from a generic template. From there, you receive weekly reporting with full visibility into how your team is performing. If something needs adjusting, we identify it and address it. You stay informed without being in the weeds.

What happens if it isn't working?

We stand behind every placement with a 90-day replacement guarantee. If the fit isn't right, we rebuild at no additional cost. That said, we stay involved throughout the engagement precisely to catch problems early — most issues are resolved long before they become decisions.

How quickly can we get started?

Most engagements go from intake to launch in two to three weeks. We move deliberately because a rushed match costs everyone more in the long run. The time we invest upfront is what makes the partnership work.

What does this cost?

Pricing is scoped to your role, the skills required, and the hours needed. We provide a transparent quote with no hidden margins — what you're quoted is what you pay. Expect rates comparable to a skilled US contractor, with the consistency and accountability of a fully managed team behind them.

Who are the professionals on your team, and where are they based?

Our professionals are based internationally, recruited specifically for your role rather than assigned from a standing pool. We pay above-market rates for the regions we hire in — not because it's the ethical thing to do, though it is, but because it's what produces the quality and retention our clients rely on. Engaged, fairly compensated professionals deliver better support. Your customers feel that difference.
