Questions

FAQ

How do payments work?

You pay Threyd directly via ACH transfer or wire. We handle all payments to your professional, including compliance with local labor laws. You receive one simple invoice monthly‚Äîno hidden fees, no surprises.

What if the professional doesn't work out?

We offer a replacement guarantee. If the match isn't right within the first 90 days, we'll find you a new professional at no additional cost. Our goal is a long-term partnership that works for everyone.

Do I need to provide equipment?

Professionals provide their own computer and workspace. For specialized software or tools specific to your business, you may need to provide access or licenses. We'll discuss your specific needs during the intake call.

What time zones do you support?

Our professionals work across various time zones. Many can accommodate US business hours (EST/PST) or work asynchronously depending on the role. We'll match you with someone whose schedule aligns with your needs.

How quickly can I get started?

Timeline varies based on role complexity, but typically 2-3 weeks from intake call to your professional starting work. We prioritize quality matches over speed‚Äîrushing leads to mismatches.

What's your pricing structure?

Pricing depends on role complexity, required skills, and hours needed. We provide transparent quotes with no hidden fees. Generally, expect rates competitive with US freelancers but with the consistency of a dedicated team member. Contact us for a custom quote.

Are professionals employees or contractors?

Professionals work as independent contractors. Threyd handles all compliance, contracts, and payments. You get the flexibility of contract work with the reliability of a dedicated team member.

Can I interview candidates before hiring?

We handle all candidate vetting and interviews to ensure quality and consistency. Our screening process is thorough‚Äîwe assess technical skills, cultural fit, and communication abilities. This saves you time and ensures you get pre-qualified professionals who match your exact needs. If the match isn't right, our replacement guarantee has you covered.
